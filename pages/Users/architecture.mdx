# Self custody

Self custody is the practice of holding your own private keys. This is the most secure way to hold your crypto assets. It is also the most difficult way to hold your crypto assets. It requires a lot of technical knowledge and a lot of responsibility. If you lose your private keys, you lose your crypto assets. There is no way to recover them. If you are interested in self custody, I recommend you read this article by Jameson Lopp.
