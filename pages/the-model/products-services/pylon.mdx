# Overview

Pylon is a service that makes it easy to route money from A to B. Pylon is network independent and can be thought of as a dynamic gateway. 
Pylon has 3 modes of operation:
    - On-Ramp: Pylon can be used to convert fiat to crypto
    - Off-Ramp: Pylon can be used to convert crypto to fiat
    - In-Network: Pylon can be used to route through and hop across crypto networks

## On-Ramp

- credit card or debit card
- ach (business only)
- wire (business only)

## Off-Ramp

- backpack credit card 
- ach
- wire

## In-Network

- paycast on farcaster
- ens
- xmtp
